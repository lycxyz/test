Team Profile

Carrying out comprehensive research and collaborative exploration is becoming a vital way for current geography research. It is of great importance to integrate various resources to conduct comprehensive simulations and experiments to understand complex geographical phenomena and processes. Aiming at solving complex geographical problems, as well as improving the theories and technologies on open geographical modelling, integration and simulation, we proposed the open framework on 'Scene-based collaboratively geographic modeling”–“Service-oriented distributed resource sharing”–“Process-rooted resource integration and simulation' and corresponding implementation scheme. The open geographic modeling and simulation platform (OpenGMS), which can be employed by multi-discipline users through network to solve complex geographic problems and conduct integrated simulation, is developed accordingly.

Mission

Provide a comprehensive geographic problem solving platform that can be used transparently, efficiently and collaboratively

Vision

Build an simulation resource integration and reuse community in a open, cooperative and win-win way

Objective

Develop open style-based geographic modeling and simulation theories and technologies
